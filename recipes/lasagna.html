Best Homemade Lasagna

A thick, meaty tomato sauce with noodles and cheese in a casserole.

Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • bell peppers
  • tomato sauce
  • tomato paste
  • crushed tomatoes
  • ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan

Steps:

  1. Make the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes
  2. Boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready
  3. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese
